About this app

🧩 Train Your Brain with Classic Sudoku!

Enjoy a clean, fast, and distraction-free Sudoku puzzle experience designed for daily brain training. Whether you are a beginner or a puzzle master, this app offers multiple difficulty levels so everyone can enjoy the challenge.

🎯 Choose Your Difficulty

Start from Noob level and work your way up to God level. Perfect for both casual players and serious Sudoku lovers.

✏️ Smart Solving Tools

Solve puzzles more easily with helpful tools:

  • Notes mode to keep track of possibilities
  • Undo option to correct mistakes
  • Mistake tracking for better learning
  • Helpful hints when you get stuck

💡 Never Get Stuck

If you run out of hints, you can earn rewarded hints and continue solving without breaking your flow.

📊 Track Your Progress

Monitor your improvement with detailed statistics and see how your Sudoku skills grow over time.

💾 Continue Anytime

Your puzzles are automatically saved, so you can continue playing anytime without losing progress.

⚙️ Simple & Clean Experience

Enjoy smooth animations, responsive controls, and a minimal interface designed for comfortable mobile gameplay.

🧠 Why Play Sudoku?

Sudoku helps improve logic, concentration, and problem-solving skills while providing relaxing and engaging gameplay.

Whether you want a quick brain workout or a long puzzle session, this app delivers the classic Sudoku experience with modern features.
